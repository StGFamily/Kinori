Feature highlights

🏡
Home
Your daily dashboard. Today's events, the star leaderboard, countdowns, and bonus stars for unexpected moments worth celebrating.
📅
Calendar
Manage your family's schedules. Day, week and month views so you can plan ahead or check what's on today.
🌅
Rhythms
Daily routines for each family member — morning, afternoon and evening. Check them off as they are completed.
✅
My Part
Each person's contributions to the family. Kids and parents earn stars for completing tasks — everyone's effort counts.
🏆
Prizes
Rewards for each family member's completed tasks. Choose and redeem rewards once enough stars have been earned.
🍽️
Meals
Weekly meal planner. Add a recipe link or nanny notes using the notes field on each meal.
🛒
Shopping
Shared list organised by store. Add items, check them off as you shop.
📝
Notes
Messages for family, nanny, or anyone who needs to know what's happening. Pin the important ones. Delete when done.
🎉
Looking Forward To
Countdowns to things your family is excited about.
⚙️
Settings
PIN-protected. Manage family members, set prizes, update passwords — everything that keeps the app running your way.
